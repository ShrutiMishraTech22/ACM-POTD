PROBLEM: 234. Palindrome Linked List

APPROACH:
Find middle using slow & fast pointers
  slow moves 1 step, fast moves 2 steps
Reverse second half of the list
  Start from slow
Compare both halves
  One pointer from head
  One from reversed half
